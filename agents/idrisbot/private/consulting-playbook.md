# Consulting Playbook

## Jae's Profile

Jae Wilson is a builder-consultant. He can research and solve any problem you point him at, but he doesn't have deep vertical experience in specific industries. He's a Domo expert with deep platform knowledge. His superpower is translating between business problems and technical solutions.

**What Jae needs from IdrisBot:**
- Industry scaffolding (KPI templates, problem patterns, forcing questions)
- Structured thinking frameworks
- Challenge his assumptions and reframe problems
- Engagement methodology and delivery structure
- Pricing and positioning guidance

## Engagement Types

### 1. AI Chief of Staff (AI CoS)
Jae's flagship offering. See [[agents/idrisbot/private/ai-cos-framework]] for the full framework.

### 2. Domo Optimization
Leveraging Jae's deep Domo expertise. Health checks, performance tuning, architecture review.

### 3. Data Architecture Advisory
Strategic guidance on data platform decisions. Often a precursor to AI CoS.

### 4. Custom Development
Building specific solutions (pipelines, dashboards, integrations). Typically scoped projects.

## Positioning Principles

- **Don't sell technology, sell outcomes** — "We reduce your monthly close from 12 days to 3" not "We implement AI agents"
- **Start with the 2am problem** — what keeps the executive team up at night
- **The wedge creates the platform** — solve one painful problem first, expand from there
- **Reference everything** — every engagement should produce a case study (with permission)
- **Pricing should reflect value, not time** — if the client saves $500K/year, don't bill $150/hour

## Forcing Questions by Engagement Type

### AI CoS
- "What would your team do with 20 hours per week back?"
- "If you had perfect information about [X], what decision would you make differently?"
- "What's the one report your CEO asks for that takes 3 days to produce?"

### Domo Optimization
- "How long does your most important dashboard take to load?"
- "What breaks when you add a new data source?"
- "Who can't get access to the data they need?"

### Data Architecture
- "What's the one data source you wish you had but can't get?"
- "How many systems does your data live in?"
- "What's the cost of a data error?"
